My story

I used to chase quick fixes — until my body made me stop

For most of my twenties, I did what most of us do. I followed the trends, counted the calories, and bought whatever the latest wellness brand told me I needed. I was doing everything "right" — and yet I was exhausted, my skin was dull, my gut was a mess, and I felt constantly disconnected from myself.

The turning point came when I hit a wall. Chronic fatigue. Hormonal imbalances. A body that felt like it was working against me. Conventional medicine gave me a list of supplements and told me to reduce stress — which, if you've ever been told that while burning out, you'll know is spectacularly unhelpful.

"The body doesn't need to be fixed. It needs to be understood, nourished, and listened to."

So I started learning. I trained in nutritional therapy, studied functional medicine, and spent years piecing together how the body actually works as a whole — not as a collection of separate problems to be managed. I discovered that almost everything I'd been experiencing traced back to a few root causes: gut dysfunction, chronic dehydration, collagen depletion, and a lifestyle that was pulling from reserves it never had time to refill.

Today, I'm here to share everything I've learned — in a way that's grounded in science, but delivered with warmth, nuance, and deep respect for the fact that every body is different. Glow isn't about perfection. It's about understanding yourself well enough to make choices that genuinely serve you.
